Our Experts

At Ottawa Pet Services, we pride ourselves on having a team of highly skilled and dedicated experts. Our professionals are passionate about providing exceptional care for your pets and ensuring their well-being. With their extensive knowledge, experience, and commitment, our experts work diligently to meet the unique needs of each animal that comes through our doors. You can trust that your beloved companions are in capable hands, receiving the highest standard of care from our team of experts at Ottawa Pet Services.

Explore the options below to find the specialized care and attention your furry friend deserves.

Dr. Lydia Anderson, Veterinarian

Lydia Anderson, the devoted and trusted main veterinarian at Ottawa Pet Services for over two decades, has become an invaluable asset to the practice through her extensive experience and unwavering commitment to animal well-being. Dr. Anderson's passion for delivering exceptional care to every furry patient is evident in her personalized approach and genuine compassion. Pet owners depend on her expertise and steadfast dedication, knowing their cherished companions are in the most capable hands. With a solidified reputation as a highly skilled and knowledgeable veterinarian, Dr. Lydia Anderson has become an integral part of the Ottawa Pet Services family, further cementing her legacy.

Dr. Sophia Rivera, Chief Surgeon

Dr. Sophia Rivera, a renowned resident surgeon at Ottawa Pet Services, brings her expertise and passion for surgical interventions to the practice, serving as an invaluable member of the veterinary team. With exceptional surgical skills and a profound understanding of animal anatomy, she provides comprehensive care, ranging from routine procedures like spaying and neutering to complex surgeries, including tumor removal and orthopedic interventions. Evelyn Martin, Pet Groomer

Evelyn Martin, a skilled and devoted pet groomer, is renowned for her exceptional talent and unwavering passion in turning furry companions into picture-perfect pets.
