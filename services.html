Our Services

At our veterinary hospital, we prioritize the well-being and happiness of your beloved pets. Our dedicated team of trained professionals is committed to providing the utmost quality care for your furry companions. We possess in-depth knowledge of your pets, enabling us to recognize their health and happiness levels accurately. Our hospital is your trusted partner in ensuring your pet's health and happiness.

Discover the range of services we offer for your pet's well-being by exploring any of the services below.

Grooming

At Ottawa Pet Services, we understand the importance of maintaining your pet's overall well-being and appearance. That's why we offer exceptional pet grooming services tailored to meet the unique needs of your beloved companion. Our team of experienced groomers is dedicated to providing gentle and compassionate care while ensuring your pet looks and feels their best.

During our grooming sessions, we pay meticulous attention to every detail, from bathing and brushing to nail trimming and coat styling. We use high-quality grooming products and techniques to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable experience for your pet. Our goal is to create a relaxing environment where your furry friend can indulge in some well-deserved pampering.

Experience the joy of a well-groomed pet with Ottawa Pet Services. Schedule a grooming session today and let us help your furry friend shine with confidence and style.

Pharmacy

Our pharmacy is stocked with a variety of veterinary medications, including preventive treatments, antibiotics, pain management drugs, and specialized medications for various conditions. Whether your pet requires medication for a chronic illness, post-surgical recovery, or routine preventive care, our experienced team will ensure that they receive the appropriate medications tailored to their specific needs.

At OPS, we prioritize the safety and efficacy of the medications we provide. Our pharmacists and veterinary professionals carefully review each prescription, ensuring accurate dosages and proper administration instructions. We work closely with pet owners to educate them on the medications and answer any questions or concerns they may have.

We understand that your pet's health and well-being are of utmost importance to you, and our dedicated team is committed to providing top-quality pharmaceutical solutions.

Surgery

We specialize in providing a wide range of surgical operations for dogs and cats. Our experienced veterinary surgeons offer comprehensive care, from routine procedures like spaying and neutering to complex surgeries such as tumor removal and orthopedic interventions. We utilize the latest techniques and equipment to ensure successful outcomes and a speedy recovery for your furry friends.

Our skilled surgical team is well-versed in canine-specific procedures, including cruciate ligament repairs, hernia corrections, and dental surgeries. For our feline patients, we offer specialized care such as dental extractions, abscess treatments, and mass removals. Your pet's safety and well-being are our top priorities, and we maintain strict protocols for a sterile surgical environment, closely monitor anesthesia, and provide comprehensive pain management.

Contact us today to schedule a consultation or discuss any questions you may have.
